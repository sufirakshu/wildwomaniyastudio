About Wild Womaniya Studio

 

Wild Womaniya Studio exists for one reason:

To figure things out by building them.

Most of the ideas published here begin as questions.

What happens if you build a website entirely with free tools?

Can a useful publishing business be created without a large audience, a marketing budget, or complicated software?

What does a system look like when it is designed for real human beings instead of productivity robots?

What makes a project easy to return to after you've ignored it for three weeks?

The answers rarely arrive through theory.

They arrive through experimentation.

Wild Womaniya Studio is a collection of those experiments.

Everything published here has survived contact with real life. The systems, workflows, tools, and frameworks are tested, refined, adapted, and often used repeatedly before they are shared.

Wild Womaniya Studio follows interesting questions wherever they lead. Some experiments take a few hours. Others take months. The subject matters less than the process: asking better questions, testing ideas in practice, and sharing what proves useful.

Some experiments become free Field Guides.

Some grow into Handbooks.

Some are documented as complete Conversations with ChatGPT: How I Build Things, showing the full journey from question to implementation.

This is not a library of perfect answers.

It is a growing record of useful discoveries.

Keep what works.

Leave the rest behind.

Then go run your own experiments.
